Deal or No Deal

Game Instructions for BANKER'S OFFER VERSION

    Game Rules

  • 1. Choose your initial case. This case will remain with you until the end of the game.
  • 2. After choosing your case, you will choose and eliminate 6 cases. Opening and eliminating a case eliminates the possibility of winning that prize.
  • 3. Based on the remaining possible prizes, you will then have to make an offer to the banker in the top left box.
  • 4. The banker will decide whether to accept or reject your offer. He will reject it if your offer is too high.
  • 5. If he accepts the offer, the game ends and you will win the amount you have offered. If he rejects your offer, the game continues and rounds will proceed; you must continue eliminating cases from the board.
  • 6. Once there is 1 case left, you will have the option of choosing your case or the final case in the game. Click on the case of your choice.
  • 7. After making your decision, the case you have chosen will open and display your winnings, and the game will end.
  • 8. After the game has ended, a Game ID will be displayed at the bottom. You will need to enter this into the Qualtrics form.
  • 9. Once you have been given the Game ID, you can safely close out of this Deal or No Deal tab or click the back button so that you can return to the Qualtrics form and submit it.
